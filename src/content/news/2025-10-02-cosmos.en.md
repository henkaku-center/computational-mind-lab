Joe was a co-instructor at [COSMOS](https://cosmossummerschool.github.io/), and
gave a talk on *Understanding knowledge using Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC)
with People*
([slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/13ZtSvVbZBdnX2xwlHv62y3uT2TyBpsSQBuf0dcCkfkg/edit?usp=sharing)).

He also led a group project with six students that developed a "minimal
paradigm" for studying teaching by physical state intervention — a simplified
design that turned out to work well enough to carry the project forward into
online experiments. The code is at
[cosmos-state-interventions](https://github.com/benpry/cosmos-state-interventions).
