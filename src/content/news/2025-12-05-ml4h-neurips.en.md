Two papers led by Hongtao Hao were accepted and published in the Proceedings of
Machine Learning Research, and presented as posters at
[ML4H 2025](https://ahli.cc/ml4h/):

- *Bayesian Event-Based Model for Disease Subtype and Stage Inference* —
  inferring both which subtype of progression a patient follows and how far
  along it they are.
- *Joint Progression Modeling (JPM): A Probabilistic Framework for
  Mixed-Pathology Progression* — dropping the usual assumption that each
  patient has exactly one disease.

Both were also presented at the NeurIPS workshop on
[Learning from Time Series for Health](https://neurips.cc/virtual/2025/loc/san-diego/workshop/109560).
See the [publications page](/en/publications/) for details, and the
[disease progression project](/en/projects/disease-progression/) for context.
