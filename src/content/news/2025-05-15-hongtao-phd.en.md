Congratulations to **Dr. Hongtao Hao**, who completed his PhD in May 2025.

Hongtao's work built the lab's line of research on
[disease progression modeling](/en/projects/disease-progression/): stage-aware
event-based models, inference of disease subtypes, joint modeling of mixed
pathologies, and — most recently — [TEMPO](/en/news/2026-06-25-chil-tempo/),
which learns to do the inference with a transformer trained on the probabilistic
model's own simulations. That is a lot of ground to cover in one dissertation,
and the work has continued past it.

We are delighted for him, and glad the collaboration continues. You can find him
at [hongtaoh.com](https://hongtaoh.com/).
