During his 2022–2023 sabbatical from the University of Wisconsin–Madison, Joe
spent time as a **visiting researcher at the RIKEN Advanced Intelligence
Project** in Tokyo, and gave a talk on *Human and Machine Learning*.

The visit was a chance to work closely with colleagues at RIKEN on computational
approaches to how people and machines learn — the kind of international
collaboration a sabbatical is for.
