Joe became a **visiting researcher at the RIKEN Advanced Intelligence Project**
in Tokyo, and marked it with a talk on *Human and Machine Learning*.

In hindsight this was the beginning of a longer story: the collaborations that
started at RIKEN — including the
[COSMOS summer school](/en/news/2025-10-02-cosmos/), which RIKEN hosts — are part
of what eventually brought the lab to Japan.
