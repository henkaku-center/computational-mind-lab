[SNAFU: The Semantic Network and Fluency Utility](https://doi.org/10.3758/s13428-019-01343-w),
led by Jeff Zemla with Kesong Cao and Kimberly Mueller, was published in
*Behavior Research Methods*.

SNAFU packages the lab's methods for estimating semantic networks from verbal
fluency data into a tool other researchers can actually use — which was always
the point. See the [SNAFU project page](/en/projects/snafu/).
