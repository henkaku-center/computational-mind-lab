**Make It So (MISO) Camp** takes place on **August 24–25, 2026** at Crypto Café
in Tokyo — a two-day working session where senior practitioners from across Asia,
Chiba Tech faculty and students, and an international cohort build with AI
together and work out the methods that make it useful.

Its premise is one sentence: *you cannot delegate to a machine what you cannot
articulate.* Software engineers got good at working with AI first, not because
the programming mattered, but because writing code trains you to specify intent
precisely. Most knowledge workers have never been trained that way. The camp is
for educators, researchers, strategists, and designers who want that habit — and
who would like to be in the room while the defaults are being written.

Joe co-organizes with Noah Raford and Igor Schwarzmann, in partnership with Chiba
Tech and the Henkaku Center. More at [misocamp.com](https://misocamp.com).
