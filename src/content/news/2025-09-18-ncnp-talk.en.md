Joe gave a talk at the National Center of Neurology and Psychiatry (NCNP) on
*Understanding Disease Progression using Bayesian Event-Based Models* — how
models that infer a disease's trajectory from cross-sectional data can help
clinicians reason about where a patient is in that trajectory.
