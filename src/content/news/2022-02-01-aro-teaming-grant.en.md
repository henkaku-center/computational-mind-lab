The lab began work on *Human-Guided ML for Futuristic Human-Machine Teaming*,
funded by the **Army Research Office**, with Joe as sub-PI.

The project asks how people can guide machine learners without writing reward
functions — the question that grew into the lab's current work on
[teaching by state intervention](/en/projects/state-intervention/).
