[Communication in Action: Planning and interpreting communicative
demonstrations](/en/publications/#ho2021), led by Mark Ho with Fiery Cushman and
Michael Littman, was published in *Journal of Experimental Psychology: General*.

When you show someone how to do something, you do not simply do the task — you do
it *legibly*, in a way designed to be read. This paper formalizes that, and it is
part of the [teaching research](/en/projects/state-intervention/) the lab
continues today.
