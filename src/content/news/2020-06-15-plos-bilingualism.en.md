Congratulations to **Kendra V. Lange** and **Elise Hopman**, whose paper with
Jeff Zemla and Joe — [Evidence against a relation between bilingualism and
creativity](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0234928) — appeared in *PLOS
ONE*.

The claim that speaking two languages makes you more creative is popular and, on
this evidence, not well supported. The paper is an extended version of Kendra's
undergraduate honors thesis in the lab — one of two journal articles published
that year with undergraduate research assistants as co-authors.
