The [School of Design & Science](https://sds.chibatech.dev/) opened its doors in
April 2026: Chiba Tech's first English-language graduate program, offering
Master's and PhD degrees on the Tsudanuma campus, and the academic arm of the
Henkaku Center.

SDS is deliberately antidisciplinary — a program for people who would rather
intervene at the level of paradigms than work neatly within one. Joe is a
co-founder of the school and serves as its academic director; this lab is part
of it.

If that sounds like your kind of place, the school's
[admissions page](https://sds.chibatech.dev/apply) is the place to start, and our
[Join Us](/en/join/) page has more on what research here looks like.
