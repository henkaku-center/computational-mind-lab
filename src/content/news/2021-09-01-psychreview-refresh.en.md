[REFRESH: A new approach to modelling dimensional biases in perceptual
similarity and categorization](/en/publications/#sanbornetal2021), with Adam
Sanborn, Katherine Heller, and Nick Chater, appeared in *Psychological Review*.

Why do some pairs of dimensions feel separable — size and color — while others,
like the brightness and saturation of a patch, refuse to come apart? REFRESH
offers an account of where those biases come from.
