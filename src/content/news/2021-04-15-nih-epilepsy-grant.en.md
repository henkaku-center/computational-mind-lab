A five-year project funded by the **NIH National Institute on Aging** —
*PET/MR Correlates of Accelerated Aging in Chronic Epilepsy* — began, with Joe as
co-investigator alongside PIs Mcmillan and Prabhakaran.

The award continues the lab's work applying computational models of memory and
semantic structure to clinical populations, in collaboration with radiology and
neurology.
