Joe took part in a panel on AI, health, and disability at
**Pro-Justice: Cross-Cultural Approaches to AI Ethics**, a two-day workshop
hosted at the University of Tokyo and co-organized with the University of
Cambridge, where [Aisha Sobey](https://www.lcfi.ac.uk/people/aisha-sobey)
(Leverhulme Centre for the Future of Intelligence) convened the session.

Cross-cultural work on AI ethics is exactly the kind of conversation that gets
more interesting when the room spans institutions and countries — and it is one
the lab expects to keep having from its new home in Tokyo.
