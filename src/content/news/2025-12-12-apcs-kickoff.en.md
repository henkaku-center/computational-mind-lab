Joe presented at the Asia-Pacific Cognitive Science Kickoff Meeting, held at the
International Cognitive Science Conference 2025 — a first gathering of cognitive
scientists across the Asia-Pacific region, and a welcome one for a lab that has
just moved into it.

Several new connections came out of it, including a visit to Shizuoka University
and a new collaboration with Simon De Deyne (University of Melbourne).
