[TEMPO: Transformers for Temporal Disease Progression from Cross-Sectional
Data](/papers/files/HaoetalCHIL2026TEMPO.pdf), led by Hongtao Hao, was published
at the [Conference on Health, Inference, and Learning
(CHIL) 2026](https://chil.ahli.cc/) and appears in the Proceedings of Machine
Learning Research.

Event-based models infer biomarker progression from cross-sectional data, but
typically recover only an ordinal sequence and rely on rigid assumptions. TEMPO
trains a transformer on data simulated from the probabilistic model — keeping the
generative model where it is indispensable, while learning inference that is both
faster and more accurate than our original method.
